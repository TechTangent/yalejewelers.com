Owned by Joe Boiadjian, Yale Jewelers has been serving Santa Monica since 1978. Yale's philosophy is simple; find something that speaks to you! It should fit who you are, your personality, your style...

Joe has been in the jewelry business for over 30 years. After finishing school at GIA, he started his career working in the wholesale jewelry district in Downtown Los Angeles. As he built a successful wholesale business, he realized that something was missing… the people!

So he set out to join the retail ranks to work directly with customers to fulfill his love of creating custom pieces inspired by the client’s desire, or to delight in helping them find a unique treasure that they’ve been longing to find.

Our main goal at Yale is not only to provide our customers with beautiful jewelry, but to provide incredible service. From start to finish, Yale will be there for you. We are truly passionate about what we do. Please visit us in person to experience the difference! And don’t forget to check out our services page to see all that we offer.
